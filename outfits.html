CHARACTER REFERENCE GUIDE

What should you wear?

Pick your favorite TVD character or creature vibe. These reference cards help even if you do not know the character well.

OUTFIT + CHARACTER IDEAS

Vampires, witches, and werewolves

Vampire

Katherine Pierce

Confident, seductive, dramatic, and always unforgettable. A blood-red or burgundy gown, bold curls, statement jewelry, and regal energy fit her best.

Vampire

Stefan Salvatore

Calm, polished, and timeless. Go for a dark blazer, fitted shirt, neat hair, and a clean elegant style with a mysterious touch.

Vampire

Damon Salvatore

Dangerous, playful, and effortlessly cool. Black shirt, leather jacket, boots, dark jeans, and intense confidence are the Damon formula.

Human / Vampire Era

Elena Gilbert

Soft, emotional, and simple but beautiful. Dark dresses, boots, natural hair, and subtle styling work well for Elena-inspired looks.

Vampire

Caroline Forbes

Stylish, polished, and confident. Choose a chic dress, polished hair, clean makeup, and a bright but still dark-romantic look.

Witch

Bonnie Bennett

Magical, strong, and grounded. Velvet, dark layers, silver accessories, and a mystical aura make a perfect Bonnie-inspired style.

Hybrid

Klaus Mikaelson

Powerful and aristocratic with werewolf-vampire intensity. Long coats, boots, fitted dark pieces, and commanding presence fit Klaus.

Werewolf

Tyler Lockwood

Rugged, wild, and moonlit. Leather, darker forest shades, boots, rough styling, and strong werewolf energy work best here.
